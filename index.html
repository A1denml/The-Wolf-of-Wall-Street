The Wolf of Wall Street

The Wolf of Wall Street is about a young man named Jordan Belfort.
The movie follows Jordan through his life as he starts his own brokerage firm,
falls into a life of crime and drugs, and defrauds his clients and the government out of millions of dollars
eventually Jordan and band of brokers are investigated by the SEC,
and are ultimatley taken down and charged for their crimes

  • Leonardo DiCaprio: Jordan Belfort
  • Jonah Hill: Donnie Azoff
  • Margot Robbie: Naomi Lapaglia
  • Matthew McConaughey: Mark Hanna
  • Cristin Milioti: Teresa Petrillo
  • Jon Bernthal: Brad Bodnick
